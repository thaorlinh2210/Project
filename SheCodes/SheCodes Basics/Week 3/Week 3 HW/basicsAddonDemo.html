Salsa

My favourite hobby

Salsa is a Latin dance, associated with the music genre of the same name, which was first popularised in the United States in the 1960s in New York City. Salsa is a amalgamation of Cuban dances, such as mambo, pachanga and rumbe, as well as American dances such as swing and tap.
